Tentative Dates for 2025

  • BA / BSc / BCom (Odd Sem): Expected in December/January.
  • MA / MSc / MCom: Expected in January 2025.
  • Practical Exams: Usually start 1 week before theory exams.

How to Download Official Date Sheet?

  1. Visit the official KUK website: kuk.ac.in
  2. Look for the "Date Sheet" section on the left sidebar.
  3. Select your course and semester hierarchy.
  4. Download the PDF and print it out.
