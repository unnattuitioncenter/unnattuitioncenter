How did Kurukshetra Perform in CBSE Exams?

An in-depth look at the performance of our city's students.

The Trend: Science vs Commerce

This year, Kurukshetra saw a surge in high scorers in the Non-Medical (Science) stream. Schools like Gita Niketan produced district toppers, proving that our city is a hub for talent.

Subject-Wise Analysis

  • Mathematics: The toughest paper this year, yet students who focused on NCERT concepts scored 95+.
  • Physics: Conceptual questions tricked many, highlighting the need for better coaching.
  • English: High scoring across all schools.
