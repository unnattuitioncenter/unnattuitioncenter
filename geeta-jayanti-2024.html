International Geeta Mahotsav 2024: A Student's Guide

Experience the spirituality and culture of Kurukshetra at Brahma Sarovar.

The Pride of Kurukshetra

Every year, our city transforms during the Geeta Jayanti. For students in Kurukshetra, it is not just a mela; it is a chance to connect with our heritage.

Key Highlights for 2024

  • Deep Daan: Lighting of thousands of lamps at Brahma Sarovar.
  • Crafts Mela: Artists from across India showcasing their work.
  • Evening Aarti: The mesmerizing 6:00 PM ritual you cannot miss.

Traffic & Travel Tips

During the festival, roads near Pipli and University Third Gate get very busy. We recommend using the provided shuttle buses if you are visiting from the sectors.
