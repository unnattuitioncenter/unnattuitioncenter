Top 5 Schools in Kurukshetra: A 2025 Review

Finding the right school is hard. We break down the best options for your child.

Choosing the Right Foundation

Kurukshetra, the land of Dharma, is also becoming a hub for education in Haryana. For parents in Didar Nagar, Sector 13, and Urban Estate, selecting a school is a big decision.

At Unnat Tuition Center, we teach students from all these schools, so we know their internal academic standards very well. Here is our honest list.

1. Gita Niketan Awasiya Vidyalaya

Known for its discipline and strong cultural roots. Academically very rigorous.

Perfect for: Students aiming for competitive exams like JEE/NEET along with schooling.

2. Delhi Public School (DPS)

Provides excellent infrastructure and global exposure. Great for overall personality development.

3. Wisdom World School

Modern amenities and a focus on extra-curricular activities. Good for confident speakers.
