Best Libraries & Study Spots in Kurukshetra

Silence is golden. Here is where you can find it.

Why You Need a Library?

Home can be distracting. The TV, the guests, the phone. Serious aspirants for UPSC, HSSC, or JEE know that a dedicated reading room is essential for focus.

1. Kurukshetra University (JL N Library)

The biggest and the best. Open 24x7 for university students. A massive collection of books and a studious atmosphere.

2. District Library (Sector 10)

A government initiative. peaceful environment and very affordable membership fees.

3. Private Reading Rooms (Sector 13)

Many private libraries have opened in Sector 13 offering AC, Wi-Fi, and comfortable seating.
